VMR Training

Project Overview

Developed a training module for volunteer firefighters on the correct use of VMR radios within fire trucks during emergency operations.

The project combined step-by-step instruction, scenario-based learning, and practical communication examples to improve radio procedures, coordination, and operational safety.

Purpose...

A learning module to instruct firefighter how to use VMR radios

Built with...

Flash, Adobe Illustrator

01

The Problem

Volunteer firefighters needed to learn correct VMR radio protocols for fire trucks but had limited access to hands-on equipment training.

02

The Process

Built an interactive Flash module with illustrated radio interfaces and scenario-based exercises to simulate real operational conditions.

03

The Outcome

Volunteers could practise radio procedures before deployment, reducing communication errors in the field.
